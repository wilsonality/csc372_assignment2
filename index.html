About me!

Why Travel?

Traveling is the my soul's way of communicating with the world. My love for this planet is expressed through witnessing it in all of its glory, one viewpoint at a time. I know that not all are able to travel to the extent that I do, so I document my excursions here for all to see. Come with me as I travel the world, and stay tuned for the advice and wisdom that I learn along the way.

Stay curious!
